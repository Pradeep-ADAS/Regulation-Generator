[Requirements Engineering Toolkit] Rule-based pipeline for extracting and structuring requirements from safety-critical regulatory documents to support traceability and systems engineering analysis.

⭐ **1. Introduction**

Safety-critical automotive and aerospace systems rely on requirements management tools such as IBM DOORS, Jama Connect, and Polarion to ensure traceability, validation, 
and compliance across the system lifecycle. However, regulatory documents feeding these tools are often large, unstructured, and require significant manual effort to convert into structured requirements.

This project automates the pre-processing stage of requirements engineering, acting as a bridge between raw regulatory documents and formal requirements management tools. It extracts, classifies, and structures requirements from regulatory text, producing a 
structured dataset ready for import into tools like DOORS or Polarion for further analysis and traceability.
